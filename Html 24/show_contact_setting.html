Show Contact detail settings

We will show your contact details to students without using your coins. Students are required to be logged in and must have coins to get access your contact details.
Paid contact sharing allows you to share your contact details with students easily. They can see your contact details by giving limited or no information. Students can see your contact details even when not logged in.

The coins required to share your contact details are calculated from the student's location based on their phone number (if available) and IP address.

At the time of writing this (May 1, 2020), you can expect to spend an average of 50 coins for showing your contact to a student.

It's still free to receive messages from the students.

Coins are not refunded, ever, whatever be the case. If you decide to share your contact details directly, you are taking that risk.

Due to the limitations of the internet itself, this system can't be perfect. We still believe it will yield useful queries and will be profitable in the long term.

You also get a lot of options to manage how and with who you share your contact details.

If you are not comfortable sharing your contact details with strangers, you shouldn't turn on this option.

There are plenty of very good reasons:

  • Like all popular websites, a lot of marketers, spammers, and competitors visit us. You may end up paying for them to contact you.
  • An unidentifiable stalker may have all your contact details.
  • We use IP address to find the location of the user. This method is not 100% reliable. You may end up paying more coins for a student depending on the location we get. You will pay less in some cases as well.
  • There is a chance that you may not receive a call from students who see your number.
